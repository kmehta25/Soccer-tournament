ABOUT US


The SER515 Soccer Tournament was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate teenagers.
Our Soccer Tournament is regarded as one of a select set of tournament clubs with a really international reputation.
We take pleasure in providing the finest possible atmosphere for young players' growth as footballers and as people.

The chairman, Mike, has been a soccer fan before his parents were born. When he turned 6, he received an invitation to become the world's youngest National Soccer League ambassador. At the age of 7, he coached a team of players at the average age of 8 who miraculously have already won 9 matches in a row since then.


Our tournament is expected to have no more than 750 teams in age ranges from 8 years old (U8) through over 18 (18+) and O48. We have a unique ideology that is well-known among our players, staff, and supporters, is recognized across the world, and distinguishes us from other football tournaments. Team spirit, hard effort, dedication, and the capacity to overcome any hurdles in life, as well as the ability to play with dignity and fairness, are all qualities we want in our players.
